## 1. Introduction & Definition
* **Level:** Level III Newborn Care facility.
* **Location:** District Hospitals (DH) or sub-district hospitals.
* **Criteria:** Mandatory for any health facility conducting **>3,000 deliveries per year**.
* **Scope:** Provides care to sick newborns (including low birth weight and preterm) but excludes those requiring mechanical ventilation or major surgery.

## 2. Location and Design
* **Proximity:** Distinct area close to the Labor Room/Operation Theatre to minimize transport time.
* **Environment:**
    * **Temperature:** Maintained between **26°C – 28°C** (78.8°F – 82.4°F).
    * **Lighting:** Well-illuminated with cool white fluorescent/LED lights; shadow-free.
    * **Access:** Controlled entry to prevent infection; no passage for general traffic.

## 3. Planning and Space Requirements
* **Bed Strength Calculation:**
    * **Intramural:** 3 beds per 1,000 annual deliveries.
    * **Extramural:** Add 30% buffer for outside referrals.
    * *Example (3000 deliveries):* 9 intramural + 3 extramural = 12 Beds.
* **Unit Size:** Minimum **100 sq. ft. per bed** total.
    * **Clinical Area:** 50 sq. ft. per bed.
    * **Ancillary Area:** 50 sq. ft. per bed (Nursing station, storage, clean/dirty utility).
* **Hand Washing:** Elbow-operated stations within 20 feet of every bed.

## 4. Zoning
* **Baby Care Area:** Divided into interconnected rooms (e.g., isolation, preterm) separated by observation windows.
* **Ancillary Areas:**
    * Gowning area at entrance.
    * Clean utility (medication/fluid prep).
    * Soiled utility (waste holding).
    * Side laboratory.
    * Mothers' area (for breastfeeding/KMC).

## 5. Staffing (Standard 12-Bed Unit)
* **Physicians:** 3 (to ensure round-the-clock cover).
* **Staff Nurses:** 10 (Minimum 2 per shift; accounts for leave reserve).
* **Support Staff:** 4 (Cleaning and hygiene).

## 6. Essential Equipment
* **Thermoregulation:** Radiant warmers (Open care systems).
* **Respiratory:** Oxygen concentrators, hood, nasal prongs, pulse oximeters.
* **Jaundice Mgmt:** Phototherapy units (single/double surface).
* **Fluid Mgmt:** Infusion pumps/Syringe pumps.
* **Others:** Resuscitation kits, electronic weighing scales, suction pumps.
