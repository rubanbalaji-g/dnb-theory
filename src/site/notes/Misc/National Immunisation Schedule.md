**Universal Immunisation Programme (UIP) - Ministry of Health & Family Welfare, Government of India**  
(Updated for 2026; core schedule unchanged from 2025 with nationwide HPV rollout for adolescent girls)

| Age / Group            | Vaccine                               | Dose                             | Route              | Site                    | Diseases Protected Against                                                            | Notes / Remarks                                                                                       |
| ---------------------- | ------------------------------------- | -------------------------------- | ------------------ | ----------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Pregnant Women**     | Td-1 (Tetanus + [[Infectious Diseases/Diphtheria\|Diphtheria]])       | 0.5 ml                           | Intramuscular (IM) | Upper arm               | Tetanus, [[Infectious Diseases/Diphtheria\|Diphtheria]]                                                               | As early as possible in pregnancy                                                                     |
| **Pregnant Women**     | Td-2                                  | 0.5 ml                           | IM                 | Upper arm               | Tetanus, [[Infectious Diseases/Diphtheria\|Diphtheria]]                                                               | 4 weeks after Td-1                                                                                    |
| **Pregnant Women**     | Td-Booster                            | 0.5 ml                           | IM                 | Upper arm               | Tetanus, [[Infectious Diseases/Diphtheria\|Diphtheria]]                                                               | If 2 doses received in previous pregnancy within last 3 years                                         |
| **At Birth**           | [[Misc/BCG\|BCG]]                               | 0.05 ml (up to 1 month) / 0.1 ml | Intradermal (ID)   | Left upper arm          | Tuberculosis (severe forms)                                                           | Within 24-72 hours of birth                                                                           |
| **At Birth**           | bOPV-0 (Oral Polio)                   | 2 drops                          | Oral               | -                       | Poliomyelitis (types 1 & 3)                                                           | Within 15 days of birth                                                                               |
| **At Birth**           | [[Gastrointestinal/Hepatitis B\|Hepatitis B]] (Birth dose)          | 0.5 ml                           | IM                 | Anterolateral mid-thigh | [[Gastrointestinal/Hepatitis B\|Hepatitis B]]                                                                       | Preferably within 24 hours                                                                            |
| **6 Weeks**            | bOPV-1                                | 2 drops                          | Oral               | -                       | Poliomyelitis                                                                         | -                                                                                                     |
| **6 Weeks**            | fIPV-1 (Fractional Inactivated Polio) | 0.1 ml                           | ID                 | Right upper arm         | Poliomyelitis                                                                         | -                                                                                                     |
| **6 Weeks**            | Pentavalent-1                         | 0.5 ml                           | IM                 | Anterolateral mid-thigh | [[Infectious Diseases/Diphtheria\|Diphtheria]], Pertussis, Tetanus, [[Gastrointestinal/Hepatitis B\|Hepatitis B]], Hib ([[Neurology/Meningitis\|meningitis]] & pneumonia) | -                                                                                                     |
| **6 Weeks**            | Rotavirus-1 (RVV-1)                   | 5 drops (liquid)                 | Oral               | -                       | Rotavirus diarrhoea                                                                   | -                                                                                                     |
| **6 Weeks**            | PCV-1 (Pneumococcal Conjugate)        | 0.5 ml                           | IM                 | Anterolateral mid-thigh | Pneumococcal pneumonia & [[Neurology/Meningitis\|meningitis]]                                               | Phased introduction (selected states/districts)                                                       |
| **10 Weeks**           | bOPV-2                                | 2 drops                          | Oral               | -                       | Poliomyelitis                                                                         | -                                                                                                     |
| **10 Weeks**           | Pentavalent-2                         | 0.5 ml                           | IM                 | Anterolateral mid-thigh | [[Infectious Diseases/Diphtheria\|Diphtheria]], Pertussis, Tetanus, [[Gastrointestinal/Hepatitis B\|Hepatitis B]], Hib                              | -                                                                                                     |
| **10 Weeks**           | Rotavirus-2                           | 5 drops                          | Oral               | -                       | Rotavirus diarrhoea                                                                   | -                                                                                                     |
| **14 Weeks**           | bOPV-3                                | 2 drops                          | Oral               | -                       | Poliomyelitis                                                                         | -                                                                                                     |
| **14 Weeks**           | Pentavalent-3                         | 0.5 ml                           | IM                 | Anterolateral mid-thigh | [[Infectious Diseases/Diphtheria\|Diphtheria]], Pertussis, Tetanus, [[Gastrointestinal/Hepatitis B\|Hepatitis B]], Hib                              | -                                                                                                     |
| **14 Weeks**           | Rotavirus-3                           | 5 drops                          | Oral               | -                       | Rotavirus diarrhoea                                                                   | -                                                                                                     |
| **14 Weeks**           | fIPV-2                                | 0.1 ml                           | ID                 | Right upper arm         | Poliomyelitis                                                                         | -                                                                                                     |
| **9-11 Months**        | MR-1 (Measles + Rubella)              | 0.5 ml                           | Subcutaneous (SC)  | Right upper arm         | Measles, Rubella                                                                      | -                                                                                                     |
| **9-11 Months**        | JE-1 ([[Infectious Diseases/Japanese Encephalitis\|Japanese Encephalitis]])      | 0.5 ml                           | SC / IM            | As per vaccine          | [[Infectious Diseases/Japanese Encephalitis\|Japanese Encephalitis]]                                                             | Endemic districts only                                                                                |
| **9-11 Months**        | [[Nutrition/Vitamin A\|Vitamin A]] (1st dose)              | 1 ml (1 lakh IU)                 | Oral               | -                       | [[Nutrition/Vitamin A\|Vitamin A]] deficiency                                                              | Given with MR-1                                                                                       |
| **9-11 Months**        | PCV Booster                           | 0.5 ml                           | IM                 | Anterolateral mid-thigh | Pneumococcal diseases                                                                 | Phased                                                                                                |
| **9-11 Months**        | fIPV-3 (if required in schedule)      | 0.1 ml                           | ID                 | Right upper arm         | Poliomyelitis                                                                         | As per state protocol                                                                                 |

| **16-24 Months**       | bOPV Booster                          | 2 drops                          | Oral               | -                       | Poliomyelitis                                                                         | -                                                                                                     |
| **16-24 Months**       | MR-2                                  | 0.5 ml                           | SC                 | Right upper arm         | Measles, Rubella                                                                      | -                                                                                                     |
| **16-24 Months**       | JE-2                                  | 0.5 ml                           | SC / IM            | As per vaccine          | [[Infectious Diseases/Japanese Encephalitis\|Japanese Encephalitis]]                                                             | Endemic districts only                                                                                |
| **16-24 Months**       | [[Nutrition/Vitamin A\|Vitamin A]] (2nd dose)              | 2 ml (2 lakh IU)                 | Oral               | -                       | [[Nutrition/Vitamin A\|Vitamin A]] deficiency                                                              | -                                                                                                     |
| 2-5 Years              | TCV (typhoid conjugate vaccine)       | 0.5                              | IM                 | anterolateral mid-thigh | Typhoid                                                                               |                                                                                                       |

| **10 Years**           | Td (Tetanus + [[Infectious Diseases/Diphtheria\|Diphtheria]])         | 0.5 ml                           | IM                 | Upper arm               | Tetanus, [[Infectious Diseases/Diphtheria\|Diphtheria]]                                                               | -                                                                                                     |
| **16 Years**           | Td                                    | 0.5 ml                           | IM                 | Upper arm               | Tetanus, [[Infectious Diseases/Diphtheria\|Diphtheria]]                                                               | -                                                                                                     |
| **9-14 Years (Girls)** | HPV (Human Papillomavirus)            | 1 or 2 doses (as per age)        | IM                 | Upper arm               | Cervical cancer & other HPV-related cancers                                           | **New nationwide rollout in 2026** (single-dose campaign for 14-year cohort + routine for 9-14 years) |
