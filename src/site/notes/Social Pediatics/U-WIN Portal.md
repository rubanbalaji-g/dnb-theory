## Definition
U-WIN is a comprehensive, citizen-centric digital platform designed to digitize the Universal Immunization Programme (UIP) of India. Modeled after the Co-WIN platform, it serves as a name-based electronic registry to track the immunization status of every pregnant woman and child (0–16 years) in real-time.

## Objectives
*   **Digital Tracking:** To establish a digital tracking mechanism for every pregnant woman and child to ensure no one is left behind.
*   **Elimination of Zero-Dose Children:** To identify and reach "zero-dose" children (those who haven't received the first DPT dose) and "missed" children.
*   **Anywhere Access:** To facilitate "Anywhere Access, Anytime Vaccination," particularly benefiting migrant populations.
*   **Real-time Monitoring:** To provide program managers with real-time data on immunization coverage and vaccine utilization.

## Key Features
*   **Registration & Identification:** 
    *   Self-registration via web portal or mobile app.
    *   On-site (walk-in) registration at vaccination centers.
    *   Integration with Aadhaar for beneficiary identification.
*   **ABHA Integration:** Automated generation of Ayushman Bharat Health Account (ABHA) IDs for newborns and pregnant women.
*   **Scheduling:** Online appointment booking for specific sessions and centers.
*   **Digital Certificates:** Generation of uniform, QR-based, digitally verifiable e-vaccination certificates immediately after administration.
*   **Automated Alerts:** SMS notifications for registration confirmation and reminders for upcoming/due doses.
*   **Offline Mode:** Enables vaccinators to record data in areas with poor internet connectivity, which auto-syncs once online.

## Implementation Structure
*   **Target Population:** 2.9 crore pregnant women and 2.6 crore infants annually.
*   **Vaccines Covered:** Covers all 12 Vaccine-Preventable Diseases (VPDs) under UIP (e.g., BCG, OPV, Pentavalent, Rotavirus, MR, PCV, DPT, Td).
*   **User Modules:** 
    1.  **Citizen Module:** For self-registration and certificate downloads.
    2.  **Vaccinator Module:** For recording administrative data and delivery outcomes.
    3.  **Administrator Module:** For session planning and coverage analysis.

## Benefits in Pediatric Practice
*   **Precision in Scheduling:** Adherence to minimum dose intervals is hard-coded into the system.
*   **Data Reliability:** Replaces manual, error-prone registers (ASHA/ANM diaries) with a single source of truth.
*   **Interoperability:** Records can be accessed across state borders, ensuring continuity of care for children of migrant workers.
*   **Outcome Tracking:** Records delivery outcomes (live birth/stillbirth) and birth weight, linking maternal health to neonatal records.

## Current Status (2026)
*   Nationwide rollout completed (excluding certain pilot-exempt regions).
*   Integrated as the primary digital backbone for the National Health Mission's immunization pillar.
