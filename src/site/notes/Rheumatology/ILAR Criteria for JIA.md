## Criteria

| Category                                   | Primary clinical criteria                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      | Diagnostic exclusions                                                                                                                                |
| ------------------------------------------ | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Systemic arthritis                         | <ul><li>Arthritis in one or more joints.</li><li>Fever duration at least two weeks, quotidian pattern for three days.</li><li>Requires one additional feature:<ul><li>Evanescent erythematous rash</li><li>Generalized lymphadenopathy</li><li>Hepatosplenomegaly</li><li>Serositis</li></ul></li></ul>                                                        | <ul><li>Psoriasis history.</li><li>Human leukocyte antigen B27 positive male older than six years.</li><li>Rheumatoid factor positivity.</li></ul>   |
| Oligoarthritis                             | <ul><li>Affects one to four joints during first six months.</li><li>Persistent subtype remains four or fewer joints.</li><li>Extended subtype involves five or more joints after six months.</li></ul>                                                                                                                                                         | <ul><li>Psoriasis history.</li><li>Human leukocyte antigen B27 positivity.</li><li>Rheumatoid factor positivity.</li><li>Systemic disease.</li></ul> |
| Polyarthritis (Rheumatoid factor negative) | <ul><li>Involves five or more joints during first six months.</li><li>Rheumatoid factor tests negative.</li></ul>                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              | <ul><li>Psoriasis history.</li><li>Human leukocyte antigen B27 positivity.</li><li>Systemic disease.</li></ul>                                       |
| Polyarthritis (Rheumatoid factor positive) | <ul><li>Involves five or more joints during first six months.</li><li>Two positive rheumatoid factor tests required.</li><li>Tests performed at least three months apart.</li></ul>                                                                                                                                                                            | <ul><li>Psoriasis history.</li><li>Human leukocyte antigen B27 positivity.</li><li>Systemic disease.</li></ul>                                       |
| Psoriatic arthritis                        | <ul><li>Arthritis and psoriasis present.</li><li>Alternatively, arthritis plus two features:<ul><li>Dactylitis</li><li>Nail pitting or onycholysis</li><li>Psoriasis in first-degree relative</li></ul></li></ul>                                                                                                                                              | <ul><li>Human leukocyte antigen B27 positivity.</li><li>Rheumatoid factor positivity.</li><li>Systemic disease.</li></ul>                            |
| Enthesitis-related arthritis               | <ul><li>Arthritis and enthesitis present.</li><li>Alternatively, arthritis or enthesitis plus two features:<ul><li>Sacroiliac joint tenderness</li><li>Inflammatory lumbosacral pain</li><li>Human leukocyte antigen B27 antigen</li><li>Onset in male older than six years</li><li>Acute anterior uveitis</li><li>Relevant family history</li></ul></li></ul> | <ul><li>Psoriasis history.</li><li>Rheumatoid factor positivity.</li><li>Systemic disease.</li></ul>                                                 |
| Undifferentiated arthritis                 | <ul><li>Fulfills criteria for no single category.</li><li>Alternatively fulfills criteria for two or more categories.</li></ul>                                                                                                                                                                                                                                | <ul><li>None</li></ul>                                                                                                                               |
