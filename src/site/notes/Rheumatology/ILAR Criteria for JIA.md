## Criteria

|Category|Primary clinical criteria|Diagnostic exclusions|
|---|---|---|
|Systemic arthritis|Arthritis in one or more joints. Fever duration at least two weeks, quotidian pattern for three days. Requires one additional feature: evanescent erythematous rash, generalized lymphadenopathy, hepatosplenomegaly, or serositis.|Psoriasis history. Human leukocyte antigen B27 positive male older than six years. Rheumatoid factor positivity.|
|Oligoarthritis|Affects one to four joints during first six months. Persistent subtype remains four or fewer joints. Extended subtype involves five or more joints after six months.|Psoriasis history. Human leukocyte antigen B27 positivity. Rheumatoid factor positivity. Systemic disease.|
|Polyarthritis (Rheumatoid factor negative)|Involves five or more joints during first six months. Rheumatoid factor tests negative.|Psoriasis history. Human leukocyte antigen B27 positivity. Systemic disease.|
|Polyarthritis (Rheumatoid factor positive)|Involves five or more joints during first six months. Two positive rheumatoid factor tests required. Tests performed at least three months apart.|Psoriasis history. Human leukocyte antigen B27 positivity. Systemic disease.|
|Psoriatic arthritis|Arthritis and psoriasis present. Alternatively, arthritis plus two features: dactylitis, nail pitting or onycholysis, psoriasis in first-degree relative.|Human leukocyte antigen B27 positivity. Rheumatoid factor positivity. Systemic disease.|
|Enthesitis-related arthritis|Arthritis and enthesitis present. Alternatively, arthritis or enthesitis plus two features: sacroiliac joint tenderness, inflammatory lumbosacral pain, Human leukocyte antigen B27 antigen, onset in male older than six years, acute anterior uveitis, relevant family history.|Psoriasis history. Rheumatoid factor positivity. Systemic disease.|
|Undifferentiated arthritis|Fulfills criteria for no single category. Alternatively fulfills criteria for two or more categories.|None|
