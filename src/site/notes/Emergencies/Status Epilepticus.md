# #definition 
### ILAE defintion
- t<sub>1</sub> time at which treatment should be initiated
- t<sub>2</sub> time at which seizure can lead to long term sequalae

- For GTCS, status epilepticus is continuous convulsive activity without regaining consciousness (t<sub>1</sub> = 5 min, t<sub>2</sub> ≥ 30 min)
- For focal seizure with impaired awareness (t<sub>1</sub> = 10 min, t<sub>2</sub> = 30 min)
- for absent seizure epilepticus  (t<sub>1</sub> = 10-15 min, t<sub>2</sub> = unknown)

## Epidemiology
- febrile SE is most common SE in children
- 14% risk of neurological deficits
- 4-5% risk of mortality
